## 📖 Description

Warehouse Management Systems (WMS) are a common technology to automate warehouses, inventory management, and other supply chain functions during digital transformation. WMS systems are commonly used by distribution, retail, and manufacturing companies throughout the world. There are plenty of WMS systems to choose from, but all have distinct strengths, weaknesses, and tradeoffs.

This video outlines our independent ranking of the top 10 WMS software in the industry today. This ranking was compiled based on our team's experience and research with leading WMS systems. No vendors influenced or compensated us for this ranking.

WMS systems in this ranking include:

-SAP S/4HANA
-Highjump
-Manhattan Associates
-Oracle SCM Cloud / WMS Cloud
-Microsoft Dynamics 365
-Infor SCM / CloudSuite
-Soften
-3PL Central
-Oracle NetSuite
-Fishbowl
