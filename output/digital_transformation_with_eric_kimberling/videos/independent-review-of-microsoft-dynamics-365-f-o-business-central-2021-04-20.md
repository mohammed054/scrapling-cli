## 📖 Description

Microsoft Dynamics 365 is one of the leading ERP systems in the market. Many larger organizations are beginning their journeys from legacy Dynamics ERP products such as Great Plains (GP), Axapta (AX), and Navision (NAV), while others are migrating from non-Microsoft products.

Microsoft Dynamics 365 is a great fit for some organizations, but not all. This independent review provides an overview of the strengths and weaknesses of this ERP software. It also provides a breakdown of the difference between Dynamics Business Central and Finance and Operations (F&O). 

NOTE: Third Stage Consulting is not affiliated with Microsoft or any other ERP vendors. This review was done completely independently without the influence of or payment from Microsoft.
