## 📖 Description

As we plan for 2022 and beyond, it’s time to rethink our supply chain strategies.  We need to think about how supply chains have worked in the past and how we need to adjust them to be successful now and in the future. Procurement, logistics, warehouse management, transportation management, and other aspects of the supply chain need to be reimagined for the future. 

In this video I’ll cover the Top 10 supply chain strategies and trends for 2022 to help you and your organization navigate today’s realities. These cover everything from the strategy, operations, organizational, and technology aspects of supply chain management.
