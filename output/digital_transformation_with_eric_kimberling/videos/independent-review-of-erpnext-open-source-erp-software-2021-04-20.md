## 📖 Description

Though many have not heard of it, OpenERP is one of the leading and emerging ERP systems for small- and mid-size businesses. This open source ERP solution can be a good alternative to NetSuite, Quickbooks, Odoo, and other systems that focus on smaller organizations. This video provides an objective and unbiased overview of the strengths and weaknesses of this enterprise solution.
