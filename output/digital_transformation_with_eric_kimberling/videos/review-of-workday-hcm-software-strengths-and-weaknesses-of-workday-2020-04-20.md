## 📖 Description

Workday is one of the leading HCM systems in the market. But like any HCM or ERP option, it has its strengths and weaknesses when compared to other options.

This video provides an independent review of Workday, including an analysis of how it compares to other HCM systems. Some of the areas assessed include: 

-General functionality
-Maturity of Workday's HCM technology
-Workday ecosystem 
-Track record of recent Workday implementations

This video was not commissioned by Workday or any other vendor, so it provides a completely technology-agnostic and unfiltered view of the pros and cons of Workday.
