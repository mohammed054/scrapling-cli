According to market share estimates, SAP S/4HANA, Oracle ERP Cloud, and Microsoft Dynamics 365 are the three top ERP systems in the global enterprise tech industry. This video outlines the key differences between these three systems, as well as how to evaluate each of them relative to your unique business needs. These are the things to consider as part of an objective and technology-agnostic ERP evaluation process.
