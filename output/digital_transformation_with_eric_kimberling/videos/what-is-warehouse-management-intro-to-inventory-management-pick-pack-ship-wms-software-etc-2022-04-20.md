## 📖 Description

Warehousing is one of the most complex and critical components of supply chain management.  Understanding warehouse management and the systems that are available to help automate warehouse functions is important to effective supply chains. Functions such as pick pack ship, cycle counts, inventory management, and WMS software are just some of the key concepts to be aware of. 

In this video I’ll talk about what exactly warehouse management is and the technologies that are available to help. I will also address how warehouse management software can help enable more effective warehousing strategies.
