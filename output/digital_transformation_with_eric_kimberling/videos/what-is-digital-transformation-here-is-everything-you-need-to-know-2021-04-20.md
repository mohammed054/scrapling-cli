## 📖 Description

Digital transformation is a common and overused buzzword. But what does it mean and how does it apply to your organization? This video explains everything you need to know as a starting point.

Simply put, digital transformation is deploying any sort of technology to automate your business. This could include any one or more of the following technologies:

-ERP software (enterprise resource planning)
-CRM software (customer relationship management)
-HCM software (human capital management)
-SCM (Supply Chain Management) software
-Business Intelligence
-Machine learning
-Artificial intelligence
-Predictive analytics
-Robotics
-Industry 4.0
-internet Of things (iOt)

Digital transformation entails major changes to your business processes and people - which is even more significant than the technology itself. In other words, it is a broader business transformation. This video explains what you need to know to define what digital transformation means for your organization.
