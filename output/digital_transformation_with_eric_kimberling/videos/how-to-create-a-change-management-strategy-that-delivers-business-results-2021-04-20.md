The days of intangible change management niceties like "awareness" and "engagement" are over. Perceived silver bullets like PROSCI certification and outdated change methodologies are not nearly enough to navigate the realities of the 2020s. Today's post-pandemic realities instead require change management that delivers tangible business results.

This video outlines the things you should be doing to incorporate effective change management strategies into your business transformation. These tips and tactics are important for any ERP or HCM implementation - or even non-technology-based transformations as well.
